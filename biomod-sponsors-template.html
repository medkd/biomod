    NCKU was established in 1931 and located in Tainan City, the ancient capital in Taiwan from 1624 to 1887. NCKU has 9 colleges and offers Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Ph.D. programs. It is a research-oriented comprehensive university with integrated academic fields and earns reputation as an international top-tier university in Taiwan.

    NCKUH, national Cheng Kung university hospital, is established in 1988. The mission of NCKUH is to protect the health of the people of southern Taiwan, heading to be the international clinical research and medical education main association.
